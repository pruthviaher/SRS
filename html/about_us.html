About Shri Ram Srushti

Welcome to Shri Ram Srushti, located in the sacred village of Chas(Nali), known for its deep connection with the Ramayana. Legend has it that when Lord Ram shot his divine arrow while chasing the golden deer, it landed in the Godavari River, forming a unique rocky formation in the village, which is now called "Nali." This blessed place has been a symbol of devotion and faith for generations.
